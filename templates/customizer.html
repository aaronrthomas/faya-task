The Technology

How the Rendering Works

01

Analysis

Canny edge detection and Hough transforms measure surface tilt. Difference-of-Gaussians extracts a luminance displacement map from fabric wrinkles.

02

Perspective Warp

A 3×3 homography matrix — computed from the print area and detected tilt — transforms the design to match the product's surface angle exactly.

03

Fabric Conformation

Each design pixel is displaced using cv2.remap() with the fabric's displacement map, making it flow naturally around every wrinkle and fold.

04

Realistic Blending

Multiply blend passes fabric texture through the design. A soft-light luminance pass re-integrates the product's natural lighting so it looks truly printed.
